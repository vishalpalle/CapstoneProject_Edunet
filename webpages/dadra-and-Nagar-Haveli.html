Dadra and Nagar Haveli

Bindrabin

Bindrabin is said to be one of the holiest places in Dadra and Nagar Haveli. This place is home to the famous Bindrabin temple. It is situated near the banks of the river Sakartod. The Deity of this temple is Lord Shiva. The temple is visited by plenty of tourists regularly.

Church of Our Lady of Piety

Church of Our Lady of Piety is one of the oldest Portuguese churches standing in the region around the Church was damaged by an earthquake that shook this area back in 1912. It was closed for renovation and was again opened in June 1924. It is presently managed by the society of Pila of Silvassa.

Khanvel

Being located at only distance of 25 kms from Silvassa, Khanvel is also most visited by the travelers to Dadra and Nagar Haveli. The main alluring features of this wonderful place is greenery and mind-boggling water sports. Tribal families of Khanvel have kept their culture quite alive till today by wearing the same traditional clothes and way of living. The must-see places of Khanvel are the Church of Our Lady of Piety, the Brindaban temple, Van Vihar Tourist Complex, etc.
