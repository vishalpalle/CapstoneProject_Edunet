Hauz Khas

Lived in Delhi and are now searching for “beautiful places near me?”Hauz Khas Village is where modernisation meets history. The Hauz Khas Fort is a major fort and contains the tombs of royalties from the 14-16th centuries. The rulers of the Tughlaq dynasty have found their resting places here. The Hauz Khas Village is also the city’s hub of nightlife. Numerous posh nightclubs, bars, pubs, and restaurants dot the entire place.

Chandni Chowk

Chandni Chowk is one of the tourist places in Delhi which boasts of its spectacular markets. If you are tired of visiting monuments, you can simply visit this historic bazaar with a variety of old shops. The Chandni Chowk Bazar has ample shops for a good shopping spree. You will find beautiful displays of fresh masalas, species, clothes, jewels, etc.

Akshardham Temple

The Hindu Akshardham temple is comparatively a new place in Delhi even though its construction and architectural style reflect past traditions. This beautiful and massive temple has intricate carvings on its walls. The temple was built with pink sandstone and marble, which gives out a calming hue for the eyes. There is a movie theatre within its complex dedicated to showing the history surrounding the construction of this Temple.

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar, the tallest Minaret in the country, is one of them. Qutub Minar was built in the 12th century, predating the Red Fort. This five-story and 70-metre Delhi tourist place is a work of art and pride of Indian architecture. The Qutub complex is also home to several other historical attractions like Quwwat-ul-Islam Masjid, tombs of Altamish, Alauddin Khalji, Imam Zamin, and the Alai Minar.

Jama Masjid

Among the most impressive Tourist Places in Delhi is Jama Masjid, an architectural marvel which also happens to be India’s largest mosque. With a capacity of holding about 25,000 people at a time, the mosque is among the first creations of Shah Jahan. It has four towers of which the southern one gives you an absolutely stunning view of the city. This architectural marvel by Shah Jahan should definitely be a part of your list among the places to visit in Delhi.
