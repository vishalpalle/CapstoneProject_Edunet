International Doll Museum Chandigarh

At the International Doll Museum Chandigarh, you will get to see dolls and puppets, & that too of more than 25 different countries. The museum was built in 1985, and has dolls from countries like Denmark, Netherlands, Germany, Korea and Spain. The museum is managed by the Indian Council of Child Welfare & there are also characters from Indian Mythology such as Ramayana. The most attractive place of this Museum is the Indian section and toy train here is simply stunning.

Sukhna Lake

The Sukhna Lake in Chandigarh is spread across an area of about 3 kilometers and it is a place where many migratory birds travel to. Some of the popular birds that can be seen here are the Crane and the Siberian Duck. It is a great place to visit if you are a bird enthusiast or a photographer. Sukhna Lake is probably the most visited place for romantic couples who visit this place.

Sector 17 Market

The 17 Sector of Chandigarh is considered to be the most youthful and bustling area of Chandigarh city. It is a great shopping destination because of all the available experiences of shopping and food. This market is considered a must-visit place on your visit to Chandigarh because of the youthful energy of all the young people walking around.

Japanese Garden

A recent addition to the list of popular places to visit in chandigarh with friends as well as family, Japanese Garden has already made a name for itself with its eye-pleasing architecture, undisturbed tranquillity and lush greenery. While Phase 1 of the project was inaugurated in 2014, Phase 2 was opened to public in 2016. The two are connected via an underground tunnel, which in itself is a sight to behold as it adorned with captivating Japanese paintings.
